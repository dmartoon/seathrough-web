Effective date: 2026-01-22

SeaThrough provides ocean conditions and estimated water clarity for informational purposes only. This Privacy Policy explains what information SeaThrough collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Summary

  • No account required. SeaThrough does not require you to create an account.
  • On-device storage. Saved locations (Favorites) and saved forecast entries (Planned) are stored locally on your device.
  • No selling / no ads. SeaThrough does not sell your personal information and does not serve advertising.
  • Data sent to providers. When you view maps or request forecasts for a location, the app may send coordinates to third-party providers to return results.

Information We Collect

  • No account data. SeaThrough does not collect usernames, passwords, or profile information because no account is required.
  • On-device app data. Favorites and Planned items you create are stored on your device (for example, using iOS local storage such as UserDefaults).

Location Information

If you grant location permission, SeaThrough may access your device’s approximate or precise location to show your position on the map and to recenter the map around you. You can use the app without granting location permission by using the map, search, and dropping pins manually.

You can change location permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

Data from Third-Party Services

SeaThrough displays maps and retrieves forecast/conditions data from third-party providers. When you request data for a specific location (for example, a dropped pin, a favorite, or a planned forecast entry), the app may send that location (coordinates) to those providers to return results for that area. Requests to these providers may also include standard network information such as your IP address and device/user agent, which is typical for internet communications.

Examples of providers

  • Mapping services (for map tiles and rendering)
  • Weather/ocean data services (for forecasts and conditions)
  • Public data sources (for buoy, tide, and related marine information)

Third-party providers handle any information they receive according to their own privacy policies.

Diagnostics

If enabled in your device settings, Apple may provide aggregated crash or diagnostic information to help improve app stability and performance. SeaThrough does not use this information to identify you.

Sharing

SeaThrough does not sell your personal information and does not share data for cross-context behavioral advertising. We share information only as needed to operate the app (for example, requesting map tiles or forecast data from third-party services) or to comply with legal obligations.

Data Retention

Favorites and Planned items remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app. Data sent to third-party providers is handled according to their own policies and retention practices.

Your Choices

  • You can use SeaThrough without granting location access.
  • You can disable location permission at any time in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete Favorites and Planned items inside the app.

Children’s Privacy

SeaThrough is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date above will reflect the latest version.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: support@seathrough.app
